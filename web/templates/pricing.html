Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an "AI call"?

Each time Opaux uses AI to score a job, tailor your CV, or write a cover letter counts as one AI call. Discovering jobs and basic tracking do not consume AI calls.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your billing page with no questions asked. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

How does overnight auto-apply work?

Pro users can enable automatic mode. Opaux discovers jobs overnight, tailors your CV to each one, and submits applications — all while you sleep. You review everything in your dashboard the next morning.

Is my data secure?

Your profile and CV data are stored securely and never shared with third parties. We use industry-standard encryption and Stripe for all payment processing.
